This comparison examines two delivery management platforms built for different business needs: Routific targets growing local delivery operations with transparent per-order pricing and strong dispatcher tools, while Onfleet has repositioned as an enterprise orchestration platform for hybrid fleets and regulated industries. The article details how pricing, route optimization, and feature availability differ significantly between the two solutions.
